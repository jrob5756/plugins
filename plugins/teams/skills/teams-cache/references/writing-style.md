# Writing Style Guide

Rules for drafting and sending messages on behalf of the user.

## Formatting

- **Never use em dashes (—).** Use alternatives instead:
  - A comma, period, or semicolon for pauses
  - A hyphen (-) surrounded by spaces for casual separations
  - Parentheses for asides
  - Rewrite the sentence into two shorter ones

## Structure

- Structure every message for **readability and clarity**.
- Prefer short, direct sentences over long compound ones.
- Break complex ideas into multiple sentences or paragraphs.
- Use line breaks between distinct thoughts.
- Front-load the key point — don't bury it at the end.
- Match the tone and formality of the conversation (casual chat vs. channel announcement).
